The Bane of My Existence

Honestly, I hated Photoshop. It was the biggest learning curve ever. It was extremely frustrating because I am the type of person that needs to be great at something right away, because otherwise I feel like an old person who needs someone younger to explain things to me and then I become painfully aware of my age and that I am eternally getting older and progressively behind on the times and the future is scary.

ANYWAY, after taking a long time to learn and after A LOT of guessing-and-checking (and a lot of cursing at the computer), I finally figured out how to make this dang poster worthy of posting somewhere. I fixed a lot of the minor issues that bothered me from the start, and even though maybe it's not super cool, I'm extremely proud of myself for just finally figuring out the basics. Like, oh my god, just figuring out how to make a layer a solid color took FOREVER!!! But honestly, now that I have these basics down I feel like I can do any project now (which, yes, I know, that is very wrong but oh well).
